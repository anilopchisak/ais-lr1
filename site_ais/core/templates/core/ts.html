TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Digital twin of the cafe


Purpose of the document


This document provides a complete set of System requirements required for implementation.

The signature of the Customer and the Contractor on this document confirms their agreement with the following facts and conditions:

  • During implementation, it is necessary to perform the work in the amount specified in this Terms of Reference.

  • All ambiguities identified in this Terms of Reference after its signing are subject to bilateral agreement between the Parties.



Objectives of the System creation


From the point of view of the creators of the System:

  • Increasing the profitability of the Watering hole cafe

  • Selection of a more suitable cafe OS based on the results of the system operation

From the client's point of view:

  • Simplify and make ordering coffee and donuts more convenient and faster

  • To make the selection of coffee and donuts more accessible based on the client's preferences



The main functionality of the System

Frontend (for clients):

  • Customer's choice of the amount of coffee

  • Customer's choice of the number of donuts

  • Receiving an order readiness notification

  • Receiving a notification about the absence of the product

  • Refusal or consent to waiting for an order

Backend

  • The ability to send requests for replenishment of goods

  • Update/change stored product data

  • Receive reports on the operation of the system
